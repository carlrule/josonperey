Welcome to our dental clinic's appointment booking system with electronic health record functionality.

1. Appointment Booking

Appointments can be booked online through our website. By booking an appointment, you agree to provide accurate information and arrive on time for your appointment.

2. Booking for Someone Else

If you are booking an appointment on behalf of someone else, please select the "FOR SOMEONE ELSE" option during the booking process. You agree to provide accurate information for the individual you are booking for and ensure their awareness of these terms and conditions.

3. Electronic Health Record (EHR)

Our electronic health record system securely stores your personal and dental health information. By using our services, you consent to the collection and storage of your health data for the purpose of providing dental care.

4. Privacy and Confidentiality

We are committed to protecting your privacy and confidentiality. Your personal and health information will not be shared with third parties without your consent, except as required by law.

5. Treatment Consent

Before receiving dental treatment, you will be required to provide informed consent. Our dentists will explain the proposed treatment, risks, and alternatives to enable you to make an informed decision.

6. Payment

Payment for dental services is due at the time of treatment unless prior arrangements have been made. We accept various forms of payment, including cash, credit cards, and insurance.

7. Cancellation and Rescheduling

There will be no cancelling or rescheduling of appointment once your appointment has been accepted.

8. Non-Refundable Reservation Fee

A reservation fee may be required to secure your appointment slot. This fee is non-refundable if the patient fails to arrive for the scheduled appointment.

9. Liability

While we strive to provide high-quality dental care, we cannot guarantee specific outcomes or results. Our liability is limited to the extent permitted by law.

10. Changes to Terms and Conditions

We reserve the right to update these terms and conditions at any time. It is your responsibility to review them periodically for changes.

OUR SERVICES

Welcome to our services section. We offer a wide range of services to meet your needs. Our team is dedicated to providing high-quality solutions to our clients. Explore our services below to learn more about what we can offer you.

Oral Prophylaxis

A dental cleaning procedure to remove plaque and tartar, preventing tooth and gum diseases.

Extraction

The removal of a damaged or problematic tooth from the mouth.

Fillings

Restoring tooth structure by filling cavities with dental materials.

Fluoride Application

The application of fluoride to strengthen tooth enamel and prevent decay.

Periodontal Treatment

Procedures to treat gum diseases and maintain healthy gums.

Endodontic Treatment

Root canal therapy to save a diseased or infected tooth.

Orthodontic Treatment

The correction of misaligned teeth and jaws using braces or aligners.

Prosthodontic Treatment

Restoration and replacement of missing teeth with dentures, bridges, or dental implants.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Enhancing the appearance of teeth through procedures like teeth whitening, veneers, and dental bonding.
